Mohammad Gharib Ali Al Tamimi Cont. Est. is one of the leading general contractors in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, specializing in Civil/Structural, Earth Work, Road & Asphalt Pavement, Concrete Repairing, Waterproofing, Mechanical, Piping, Electrical, Substation Works, General Maintenance, Heavy Equipment Supply & Manpower supply.

Our dedicated team invests time and resources into understanding the unique requirements of each project, allowing us to deliver effective construction solutions that meet our clients' needs and exceed their expectations.

With extensive experience across various sectors, our commitment to quality craftsmanship and professional service has enabled countless clients to realize their construction goals — resulting in successful project completions and lasting structures.

HYC System · MEP Works

Heavy Equipment Supply

Manpower Supply

Construction Finishing
